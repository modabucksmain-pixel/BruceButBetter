# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want BruceButBetter to be a welcoming, harassment-free space for everyone contributing to or
learning from the project, regardless of experience level or background.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, PRs, and discussions.
- Assume good faith; help newcomers.
- Keep the project's scope **authorized security testing and education** (see
  [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)). Do not request, share, or encourage unauthorized or illegal use.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing others' private information without consent.
- Soliciting help with attacks on systems, networks, or spectrum you are not authorized to test.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, commits, issues, and PRs that violate this code,
and may ban repeat or serious offenders. To report a problem, open a
[private security advisory](https://github.com/Yoursel71/BruceButBetter/security/advisories/new)
or contact a maintainer through GitHub.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/),
version 2.1.
